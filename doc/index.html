BMI

  • Body Mass Index

    Imperial BMI Formula

    Bash 

      $bmi -W 189.597 -B 69.6850

  Your body mass index is: 27
  You are 9% over your ideal weight
  Overweight

    Rails 

      require 'bmi'
  bmi = BMI.new
  data = []
  data[0]='-W 189.597'
  data[1]='-B 69.6850'
  bmi.calc(data)

  Your body mass index is: 27
  You are 9% over your ideal weight
  Overweight

    Metric Imperial BMI Formula

    Bash 

      $bmi -w 86 -b 1.77
  
  Your body mass index is: 27
  You are 9% over your ideal weight
  Overweight

    Rails 

      require 'bmi'
  bmi = BMI.new
  data = [] 
  data[0]='-w 86'
  data[1]='-b 1.77'
  bmi.calc(data)

  You are 9% over your ideal weight
  Overweight

      gem install newgem
